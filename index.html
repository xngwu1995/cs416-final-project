The World Country Changes Through 1800 to 2014 Years

The d3.js data visualization project
Xuguang Wu

This visualization is an interactive slideshow which takes the user through the Dataset of the world country.
The dataset itself contains the population, income and life expectancy through 1800 to 2014 across the globe.

Overview of the dataset

Scene 1

At this scene, you could find changes through 1800 to 2014 years in different fields in different countries.
Customer can also choose different continents that they would like to have a look. The user can also slide the date slide and choose the specific date that they want to have a look.
